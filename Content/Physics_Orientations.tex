Orientations of Radiation:

$ \alpha = $ irradiaton angle, incoming radiation direction, from source/sender \footnote{Einstrahlungswinkel zur Normalen auf Grenzfläche des Mediums}

$ \beta = $ refraction angle for bented transmitted radiation $A_{t}$ orientation in matter \footnote{Durchstrahlungsswinkel zur Normalen aus Grenzfläche ins Medium}

$ \vartheta = $ reflection angle for redirected radiation $A_{r\parallel}$ parallel to $\alpha$ from matter \footnote{Abstrahlwinkel zur Grenzfläche des Mediums}

$ \theta = $ diffraction angle for redirected radiation $A_{r\perp}$ orthogonal to $\alpha$ from matter \footnote{Beugungswinkel durch Objekt ins Vakuum}

$ \varpi = \beta_1 + \beta_2 = $ angle of converging body surfaces (point tip of prismic medium), spread angle of prism

$ \varsigma = $ deviation angle of redirected ray beam after refraction (transmission) and difraction

$ u_g = $ aperture angle (acceptance) from sender source (at distance $g$)

$ w_b = $ inclination angle at observer receiver (at distance $b$)

$ \varepsilon = $ visual angle from observer/receiver (of eye)
