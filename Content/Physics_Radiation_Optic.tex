\textbf{Optical Radiation}: \index{Optical Radiation}

Deviation angle of ray beam after refraction (transmission) and difraction:
\newline \vspace{1ex}
$
\varsigma = 
\alpha + arcsin \left[ sin(\varpi) \sqrt{\eta^2-sin^2(\alpha)} - cos(\varpi)sin(\alpha) \right] - \varpi = 
\alpha + \alpha_2 - \varpi
$
\newline
where
$
\varpi = \beta + \beta_2 = const.
$
\newline \vspace{1ex}

Minimal aberration (minimal prejection error):
\newline \vspace{1ex}
$ \varsigma = \varsigma_{min} \Longleftrightarrow $
$
\varsigma_{min} \Longleftrightarrow \\
\frac{d}{d\alpha} \varsigma = 0
\ \wedge \\
\varsigma_{min} = 
2\alpha - \varpi = 
2(\alpha - \beta)
$
